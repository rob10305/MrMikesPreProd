How It Works

1

Contact MrMike

Reach out via email at mrmike@mrmikes.com or call 941-922-5070. Provide your name and the recipient's name.

2

Specify the Amount

Choose any dollar amount starting at $50. An authentication number will be assigned to your certificate.

3

Receive Your Certificate

The personalized certificate with names, dollar amount, and authentication code will be emailed to you for printing. A duplicate is kept on file at MrMikes.

4

Give the Gift

Print the certificate and present it in a card. Watch their face light up!

5

Redemption

When the recipient places an order, all gift certificates in their name are applied. If the total exceeds the order, excess dollars are refunded to the customer.
