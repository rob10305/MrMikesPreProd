MG Upholstery Kits

For MGB and MGB-GT - Fiero & Miata seats fit perfectly

Seat Fitment Guide

Fiero & Miata seats both fit well in the Mark II MGB as they are both low and narrow but comfortable for the average person.

Miata seats fit better into the Mark I MGB due to the wider transmission tunnel.

Slight modification of the seat track is required.

Replace the standard plastic handle with Leather Recliner Tabs.
