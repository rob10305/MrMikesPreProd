What is Door Welt?

Welt (also called piping or cording) is the trim that runs along the edges of door panels, seat covers, and interior trim pieces. Over time, factory welt can crack, peel, and look worn. TrimBright welt is an easy, affordable fix that also lets you add a custom color accent to your interior.
