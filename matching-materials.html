MrMike's Recommendations

Use Leather-ette For:

  • Dashboards
  • Door panels
  • Large flat surfaces
  • Non-contact areas

Less expensive and works great on large parts where you won't be in direct contact.

Use Leather For:

  • Seats (included in kits)
  • Armrests
  • Console lids
  • Shift boots

Best for "contact points" - places where occupants touch the interior.

About Natural Leather

Leather is a natural product shaped like a cow hide - not a nice rectangle. Natural hides may contain flaws like brand marks, bee stings, and other imperfections.

When you order cut pieces from MrMike, he works around these flaws to provide you with perfect, usable pieces that match your seat kit exactly.

How to Order Matching Materials

  1. Measure your parts or create paper patterns
  2. Contact MrMike with your measurements and color requirements
  3. For leather cut pieces, mail your paper patterns to MrMike
  4. Materials will be cut to match your seat kit colors exactly
