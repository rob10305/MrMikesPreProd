Shipping Information

USA Shipping

48 States: UPS Ground with tracking number provided.

Hawaii & Alaska: USPS Air 6-10 days (contact for quote)

Canada Shipping

USPS Air 6-10 days with tracking.

No brokerage fees when shipping USPS to Canada from MrMikes.

International Shipping

USPS Air 6-10 days with tracking. Rates vary by destination.

Example rates:
Germany ~$84 | Israel ~$110 | Australia ~$118

Automated orders are shipped ONLY to the customer billing address as verified by the credit card provider. If you need to ship to a different address, please call MrMike to place your order.
