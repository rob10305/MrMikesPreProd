Fiero Carpeting

Carpet recommendations and installation tips

Where to Buy Fiero Carpet

MrMikes does not make carpets for the Fiero. Instead, we recommend buying from Automotive Interiors .com - they have a good product at a good price.

Visit Automotive Interiors

MrMike's Carpet Installation Tips

Install Over the Original Carpet

Do NOT remove the original carpet. MrMike recommends putting the new carpet down on top of the original. Here's why:

  • The original carpet works as a great sound pad
  • It properly shapes the floor of the car
  • In the Fiero, the floor is not smooth - there are varying thicknesses of foam that fill gaps and smooth out the surface
  • The sides of the console are made wider with plastic add-on panels under the carpet
  • It's much easier to install new over old, and looks better when done

Pro Tip: Skip the Jute Padding

When ordering carpet from Automotive Interiors .com, type into the comments box (or mention on a phone call):

"Please do not attach the jute padding to my carpet kit"

You'll still receive the normally-included jute padding, but they won't glue it to the back of your new carpet. When putting new carpet over old, it's best not to use the jute padding.

Gluing the Carpet

  • Pedals area: Glue the new carpet to the old for safety - use a spray contact cement type glue
  • Edges: No gluing necessary - the plastic trim holds it in place
  • Under seats: No gluing needed - once the seat is bolted back on top, the new carpet won't move

Radio Housing Tip

The radio housing is difficult to remove. Being less than a foot wide where it goes over the carpet, it's much easier to just use a putty knife to tuck the carpet under it than to remove the entire radio housing.

The Firewall Panel

The "firewall" panel behind the seats of the Fiero is not carpet but a non-woven fuzzy product called Ozite. The bulk carpet sold by ACC is too stiff to properly recover this firewall panel.

MrMike recommends: Cover this panel with vinyl instead, turning it into what looks like the door panels. This creates a clean, coordinated look.
