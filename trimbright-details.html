Installation Guide

Friction Fit - No Glue Needed

TrimBright is a continuous plastic product used to fill in gaps, trim around door panels, sunroof openings, and more. No glue is necessary - the welt just stuffs into the cracks. We call this a "friction fit."

Bending & Cutting Welt

Easy to Work With

TrimBright welt bends easily around corners and curves. Use sharp scissors or a utility knife to cut to length. The flexible plastic material holds its shape once installed.

Connecting Welt Pieces
