Common Questions

Frequently asked questions about MrMikes upholstery kits

Materials & Construction

What is meant by "Leather Seating Surface"?

"Leather seating surface" means the areas you actually sit on and lean against are genuine leather. The sides and backs of the seats (areas that don't receive direct body contact) are typically made of matching leather-ette (vinyl) to keep costs reasonable while still providing the look and feel of full leather where it matters most.

Can I order matching leather & vinyl?

Yes! MrMikes can provide matching bulk materials for your other interior projects like door panels, center consoles, armrests, and more. Contact MrMike with your needs for a quote on additional matching materials.

What's the difference between leather and leather-ette?

Leather is genuine animal hide that is durable, breathable, and develops a unique patina over time. It's used on seating surfaces for comfort and longevity.

Leather-ette (also called vinyl) is a synthetic material that closely matches the look of leather. It's durable, easy to clean, and more affordable. It's typically used on non-seating surfaces like seat backs and sides.

Compatibility & Fitment

Can I install MrMikes kit on power-lumbar seats?

Yes, MrMikes kits work with power-lumbar equipped seats. The installation process is essentially the same. Just be careful not to damage any wiring when removing and reinstalling the upholstery.

Will Fiero seats fit in my car?

Fiero seats are popular swap candidates for many vehicles including:

  • C2 and C3 Corvettes (1963-1982)
  • MGB and MGB-GT
  • Triumph TR6, TR7, TR8, Spitfire
  • Kit cars and Panteras
  • Vintage Mustangs and Camaros
  • Many other classic and sports cars

Custom mounting brackets may be required. Check our vehicle-specific pages for details or contact MrMike to discuss your application.

What year Fiero seats work best?

All years (1984-1988) of Fiero seats can be used. The 1984-1987 seats are more commonly available. The 1988 seats have a slightly different style. For most swap applications, any year will work - the mounting is the same.

Installation

How difficult is the installation?

If you can follow video instructions and use basic hand tools, you can install MrMikes seat covers. All the photos on our website show customer-installed seats. The video instructions walk you through every step in real time. Most people complete both seats in a weekend.

Should I take my seats to a professional?

We actually recommend doing it yourself! The motto is "Do-it-right / Do-it-yourself." Professional upholsterers often don't follow the included instructions and may not achieve the proper fit. When you do it yourself following the video, you'll get better results.

What if my seat foam is damaged?

Supplies and instructions are included to repair damaged seat foam (the core). It's critical to repair any damage and pad any exposed metal before installing new upholstery. Failure to properly prepare the seat core can void your warranty and cause premature wear.
