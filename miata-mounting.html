Make Your Own Adapters

If commercial adapters aren't available for your specific vehicle, you can fabricate your own mounting brackets.

  • Miata seat tracks are 14" apart (center to center)
  • Use 1/4" steel plate for adapter brackets
  • Measure your existing seat mounting points carefully
  • Test fit before final welding or bolting

For: Custom applications, kit cars, other vehicles

DIY Mounting Tips

  • Height: Ensure the seat height allows proper headroom and visibility
  • Angle: The seat back angle should feel natural - not too upright or reclined
  • Track clearance: Make sure the seat tracks have full range of motion after installation
  • Safety: Use Grade 8 bolts for all mounting points - seats are safety-critical components
  • Test fit: Always do a complete test fit before final installation or welding
